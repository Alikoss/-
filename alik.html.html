About the Club

Real Madrid FC, founded in 1902, is one of the most successful football clubs in history, with numerous La Liga titles and UEFA Champions League victories.

Club History

Real Madrid has a rich history, becoming a symbol of excellence in football. Over the decades, the club has achieved countless milestones:

  1. 1902 - Real Madrid was founded.
  2. 1956 - First UEFA Champions League title.
  3. 2000 - Named FIFA Club of the Century.
  4. 2014 - Historic "La Décima" Champions League victory.
  5. 2017 - Record consecutive Champions League titles.
